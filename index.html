Stolen Diamond found on expedition in Siberia!

The Monk Diamond is a rare jewel. The jewel was stolen from Moscow. The theft happend three years ago. Professor Bairstone and the team found the diamond in a cave - sensation! Why was the diamond hidden in a cave? Who hid it there? The Bond Brothers are the prime suspects.

Professor Bairstone is a famous explorer. Dr Day is a top scientist. She loves dinosaur fossils. Professor Bairstone and Dr Day were looking for fossils in Siberia. Dog Ernest accompanied them. The team found the stolen diamond hidden inside a remote cave. It was Ernest, who detected the jewel.
